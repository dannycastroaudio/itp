About Me


Hey! I'm Danny Castro, a Sound Designer and Audio Programmer from Costa Rica, currently based in Boston. I focus heavily on narrative and immersive sound design, trying to tell a story of not only what a player sees on-screen, but of the rich world that surrounds the main plot.

I think that I care way too much about sound, I definitely spend too much time thinking about it.

I also specialise in audio implementation, whether it be in-engine or using middleware. It is a role I deeply enjoy, it feels like putting a puzzle together! And man do I love a good puzzle.

In the recent years I have found myself working more and more as a technical sound designer than anything else.

In my leisure time, I make plugins! I've found this skill to be very helpful in games, and have made my fair share of custom reverb plugins for games I've worked on.

Some titles I've worked on (or am working on) include Unchained: Tale of Enra, The Ski Lodge Murder, and Echoes of Memory, to mention some. I am currently interning at Serious Point Games as a Technical Sound Designer, working on their upcoming title The Ski Lodge Murder!

Nevertheless, I am always in search of new opportunities and projects, so don't be afraid to reach out!

A little more about me


I was born in San Jose, Costa Rica, and music has been a part of my life since I was born. My father is a percussionist, my mother a dancer (she denies it). Playing drums was always treated as an integral part of my culture. I started playing piano at 3, on a toy piano my grandmother bought for me. I kept on playing piano my entire life, but I focused on percussion.

At 12, I enrolled in classical percussion courses at the University of Costa Rica. At 14, I got accepted into the National Music Conservatory of Costa Rica. At 19, I moved to the United States and began my studies at Berklee College of Music, where I'm currently double-majoring in Electronic Production and Design (EPD), and Game and Interactive Media Scoring (GAIMS). I am also minoring in Creative Coding, where I focus on developing plugins using juce and Csound, and doing a specialisation in Sound Design with a focus on audio implementation.
